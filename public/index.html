HOW TO PLAY

🎯 THE GOAL

Be the best liar. Fool your friends. Find the truth.

📝 EACH ROUND

  1. A trivia question appears with a blank
  2. Everyone writes a fake answer (a lie!)
  3. All answers get mixed together — including the real one
  4. Pick which answer you think is TRUE
  5. Vote for the Best Lie

💰 SCORING

  • Find the truth: +1,000 pts (×2 in R2, ×3 in R3)
  • Someone picks YOUR lie: +500 pts per person fooled
  • Pick a game decoy: -500 pts (ouch!)

🏆 ROUNDS

  • Round 1: 3 questions (×1 points)
  • Round 2: 3 questions (×2 points)
  • Round 3: 1 final question (×3 points)

🎭 TIPS

  • Make your lies sound believable
  • The best lies are funny AND plausible
  • Don't pick the most obvious answer — it's probably a lie
