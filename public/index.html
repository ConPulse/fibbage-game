📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 The Goal

Be the best liar. Fool your friends. Find the truth.

📝 Each Question

  • 1️⃣ A trivia question appears with a blank
  • 2️⃣ Everyone writes a fake answer (a lie!)
  • 3️⃣ All answers get mixed together — including the real one
  • 4️⃣ Pick which answer you think is TRUE
  • 5️⃣ Vote for the Best Lie

💰 Scoring

  • ✅ Find the truth: +1,000 pts
  • 🎭 Someone picks YOUR lie: +500 pts per person fooled
  • 💀 Pick a game decoy: -500 pts

🏆 The Game

9 questions. Most points wins. Best Liar award at the end.

🎭 Tips

  • 💡 Make your lies sound believable
  • 😂 The best lies are funny AND plausible
  • 🤔 The most obvious answer is probably a lie
